Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common patient questions.

How to book an appointment?

Create a patient account, log in, choose a doctor, then select one available slot.

Do I need an appointment before visiting?

Yes. The system is designed to organize clinic visits through appointment requests.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

You can manage your appointment from the patient dashboard according to the available actions.

Will I receive confirmation after approval?

Yes. Once a doctor approves or rejects a request, the system can notify the patient immediately.

Can doctors manage their own hours?

Yes. Doctors can update availability so patients only see the real free slots.
